A Little Bit About Me:

As an active and ambitious participant in life, many subjects and experiences fascinate me. I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with my bachelors in chemistry, and have worked in several tech start-ups and VC environments since. The scrappy world of software and technology funding has captivated me and groomed me into finding greater interests in technical roles using new skills and learned assets. In 2017, I joined the UC Berkeley Full Stack Development Bootcamp to do just that. Coupled with an innate thirst for innovation, adventure, positive environments and high spirits, I came into software because I wanted to explore tools that would allow me to build and contribute towards the rise of new technologies that create positive impact. I constantly strive to enjoy what I do and continue to spread that outlook anywhere I go. Humor is key. After all, why can't we have fun all the time? We can. (Go on with your bad self.)

I moved to the Bay Area to train, create, build and inspire. I'm always open to sharing fresh ideas and new perspectives. Feel free to message me any time to discuss any or all of these interests together. I look forward to collaborating with you all very soon!

Feel free to check out my Github Page for more information about what I've done, and what you'd like to do together on future projects!
