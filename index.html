About Me:

As an active and ambitious participant in life, many subjects and experiences fascinate me. I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with my bachelors in chemistry, and have worked in several tech start-ups and VC environments since. The scrappy world of software and technology funding has captivated me and groomed me into finding greater interests in technical roles using new skills and learned assets. In 2017, I joined the UC Berkeley Full Stack Development Bootcamp to do just that. Coupled with an innate drive for innovation, adventure, positive environments and high spirits, I began working with web dev projects to explore new tangible ways I could build and contribute to products that create positive impact.

To sum this up, if you like pina coladas, getting caught in the rain, dislike yoga, and possess half a brain, you know where to find me with all attached eccentricities. Bowchickachickachah, witcha bad self!
